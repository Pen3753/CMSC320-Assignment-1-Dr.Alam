Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: It allows multiple people to work on the same project at the same time, it also tracks changes made to the files in the repository so you can see who made what change.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging does not change the commit history and just ties together the two branches. Rebasing rewrites the history, adding one branches history to the end of the other branch.
